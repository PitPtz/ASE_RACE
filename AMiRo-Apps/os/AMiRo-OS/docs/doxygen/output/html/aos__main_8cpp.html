uint64_t _aosMainTM2U64 ( struct tm *  src)

Converter function to encode a TM value to a single unsigned 64 bit integer.

Contents of the TM struct are mapped as follows: bits |63 62|61 53|52 50|49 26|25 22|21 17|16 12|11 6|5 0| #bits | 2 | 9 | 3 | 24 | 4 | 5 | 5 | 6 | 6 | value | isdst | yday | wday | year | mon | mday | hour | min | sec | range | special | [0, 365] | [0, 6] | [1900, ...] | [0, 11] | [1, 31] | [0, 23] | [0, 59] | [0, 61] | The Daylight Saving Time Flag (isdsst) is encoded as follows: DST not in effect -> 0 DST in effect -> 1 no information available -> 2

Parameters
[in]srcPointer to the TM struct to encode.
Returns
An unsigned 64 bit integer, which holds the encoded time value.
