uint8_t amiro_bno_node::operation_mode

Operation mode of the bno055.

Common modes are BNO055_OPERATION_MODE_AMG, BNO055_OPERATION_MODE_IMUPLUS, BNO055_OPERATION_MODE_NDOF BNO055_OPERATION_MODE_AMG (no fusioned data) BNO055_OPERATION_MODE_IMUPLUS (relative orientation is calculated out of the accelerometer and gyroscope) BNO055_OPERATION_MODE_NDOF (absolute orientation is calculated)
